Naruto Card Collector!

How do I play?

*You're given a random number at the start of the game. Your goal is to click on the Naruto cards and get your total score to match this number.

Clicking on a card will add a set amount of points to your total score.

The value of each card is hidden from you until you click on it.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

Each time the game starts, the cards will get a new random value.
